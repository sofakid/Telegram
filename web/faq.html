Firegram FAQ

What's the deal?
  • Firegram is an app for Amazon's Fire OS to use the Telegram messaging service.
  • Firegram is a port of the official Telegram Android app, and is therefore Open Source, using the GPL v2 license.
  • The GPL license grants you certain rights and you are encouraged to read the license.
  • The code and license is available at: https://github.com/sofakid/Telegram
  • You can raise issues and report bugs here: https://github.com/sofakid/Telegram
What's different with Firegram?
  • Telegram's official client makes use of Google Play Services to provide paypent processing, barcode and facial recognition, and mapping. This is not available on Amazon's Fire OS, so:
    • Firegram reimplements mapping using Amazon's mapping services.
    • Payment processing and barcode/facial recognition functionality is unimplemented in Firegram.
